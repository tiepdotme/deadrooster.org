Dirty’s top songs ever

19 avril 2005

With some friends of ours, we made up a very cool game: “pick the songs that would make the best 80 minutes of music ever”.

The rules are simple:

  • don’t pick more than one song for a single artist;
  • try not to pick the best song of each of your favorite bands, just pick the best songs ever! (who cares of the band has made only one good song!!!).

Here is the first draft of my list: (that can evolve, of course)

Écouter sur Spotify
  • Autour de Lucie’s - Selon l’humeur
  • The Strokes - Last nite
  • Franz Ferdinand - Take me out
  • Pixies - Caribou
  • Pulp - Common people
  • The Beach Boys - God only knows
  • The Kinks - Milk cow blues
  • Nada Surf - Hi-speed soul
  • M - Nostalgic du cool
  • The Clash - Clash city rockers
  • David Bowie - Space oddity
  • Blondie - Atomic
  • Oasis - Fade away (the version from a War Child compilation)
  • Deep Purple - Highway Star
  • Led Zeppelin - Rock and roll
  • Björk - Bachelorette
  • The Libertines - Vertigo
  • Hot Hot Heat - No, not now
  • The Velvet Underground - I’ll be your mirror
  • Roger Glover and Butterfly Balls - Love is all
  • Blur - Song 2
  • Sleeper - Twisted

Up to you to build your own list.
